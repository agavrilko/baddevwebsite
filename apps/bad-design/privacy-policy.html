Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Bad Design handles information when you use the Bad Design mobile application.

Who we are

Bad Design is a creative tool for designing beadwork patterns. For privacy questions, please contact the developer using the contact information listed on the app store page where you downloaded the app, or through any contact method provided by the developer.

Information we collect

Bad Design does not require you to create an account and does not collect account registration information.

The app stores your created documents, patterns, bead selections, tool settings, and app preferences locally on your device so the app can provide its core functionality.

If you choose to import a PDF document, the selected PDF file and the selected palette identifier are sent to our processing server so the app can try to extract a bead pattern from that document.

How we use information

We use information only to provide app functionality, including:

  • Saving and opening your local beadwork documents.
  • Remembering app settings and tool preferences.
  • Processing PDF imports that you explicitly select.
  • Generating imported pattern previews from selected PDF files.

Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. PDF files selected for import are sent to our processing server only for the purpose of extracting a pattern for use in the app.

We may disclose information if required to comply with applicable law, legal process, or enforceable governmental request.

Data security

Data sent to the processing server is transmitted over HTTPS. Local app data is stored on your device using the platform storage available to the app.

Data retention and deletion

Documents and preferences stored locally remain on your device until you delete them in the app, clear the app data, or uninstall the app.

PDF files uploaded for import are used to process your request. We do not use uploaded PDF files for advertising or sell them to third parties.

Children's privacy

Bad Design is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer using the contact information listed on the app store page where you downloaded the app, or through any contact method provided by the developer.
